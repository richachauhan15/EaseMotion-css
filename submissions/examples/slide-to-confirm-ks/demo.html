How It Works

  • Interaction: Click the arrow button to initiate the slide. The thumb animates to the end, revealing a checkmark.
  • Keyboard: Tab to focus, then press Space or Enter to confirm.
  • Accessibility: Built with ARIA roles, screen-reader hints, and focus-visible outlines.
  • Dark mode: Automatically adapts to prefers-color-scheme.
  • Motion: Respects prefers-reduced-motion by disabling transitions.
  • Zero JavaScript: Uses CSS checkbox hack for all interaction logic.
