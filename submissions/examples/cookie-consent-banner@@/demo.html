Why Cookie Consent Matters

Cookie consent banners are legally required by privacy regulations like GDPR (Europe), CCPA (California), and LGPD (Brazil). They inform users about data collection and give them control over their privacy.

This example demonstrates three different styles of cookie consent interfaces, all built with pure CSS animations and minimal JavaScript for functionality.
